Installation Steps

1. Visit this Outlook download page in any browser, such as Microsoft Edge.

2. Click the button. The Outlook Installer will automatically offer you the best available version for your computer.

3. Click Open file in the Downloads notification on Microsoft Edge to start the process.

4. Click Update Button and wait for the process to complete.

5. Choose your Microsoft or Gmail account if you have one. If not, click Create an Outlook.com email account and click Continue.

6. Wait for your account email provider to load.

7. Click Next to proceed with privacy settings.

8. Click Yes, send optional data and click Accept.

9. Click Skip to complete the setup.

10. Your setup is successful.

11. After setup you can start the New a Mail. Contact support for any questions.
